# 🌐 Steel Export Dashboard – Power BI This interactive Power BI dashboard showcases key metrics and patterns in simulated steel export operations, modeled after my internship experience in international trade and supply chain analytics. ### 📌 Dashboard Highlights: - **Export Volume Overview**: KPIs display total exported tons and number of export orders. - **Port-Level Insights**: A geographic map visualizes departure ports and their relative shipment volumes. - **Product Composition**: A donut chart breaks down exports by steel product types. - **Client Interaction**: Clickable slicer cards enable client-specific filtering across all visuals. - **Monthly Trends**: Line charts reveal changes in shipment volume and average transit time over time. - **Date & Client Filters**: Dynamic filtering via time range and customer selection enhances analytical depth. The report is fully interactive and optimized for stakeholder monitoring, logistics insights, and client-facing reporting. > 🔎 Data used is fully simulated and intended for demonstration purposes only.
